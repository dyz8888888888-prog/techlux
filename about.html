About TechLux

Corporate Profile

About Us

Welcome to Hebei Techlux Industrial Co., Ltd. With 14 years of dedicated expertise in integrated design, production, and global sales, we have established ourselves as a premier manufacturer of modular flooring solutions. Headquartered in the vibrant commercial hub of Shijiazhuang City (Hebei Province), our strategic sales and administrative center coordinates seamless international logistics and client relations.

We are driven by a distinct corporate vision: to deliver an innovative, active lifestyle directly to consumers worldwide. By emphasizing creative hands-on installation (DIY) and adaptive functionality, our products empower families and commercial operators to transform any space into a dynamic, personalized environment.

Core Business & Product Lines

Leveraging state-of-the-art polymer formulations and manufacturing technologies, our primary product categories include:

PP Interlocking Flooring (Suspended Modular Sports Floors): Engineered explicitly for multi-sport athletic courts (basketball, tennis, pickleball). Featuring premium shock absorption, structural resilience, and all-weather drainage capability.

Premium Garage Floor Tiles: Heavy-duty modular interlocking tiles designed to withstand substantial vehicle weight, oil spills, and chemical exposure. Ideal for residential garages, workshops, and commercial detailing bays.

Automotive Accessories: Utilizing our advanced injection molding capabilities to produce high-precision automotive components and interior enhancements, offering complete spatial integration solutions.

Manufacturing Strength & Multi-Factory Infrastructure

To support rigorous global supply chain standards and ensure uninterrupted production capacity, we operate two distinct specialized manufacturing facilities within Hebei Province:

Xingtai Manufacturing Base: Optimized for large-scale production, continuous high-volume extrusion, and rapid supply fulfillment to satisfy substantial commercial orders.

Langfang Engineering Facility: Focused on advanced precision mold development, rigorous quality control, and technical research and development.

Comprehensive OEM Customization

Backed by 14 years of end-to-end industry integration, we fully welcome and support OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) requests. From custom brand logos and unique color matchings to proprietary structural dimensions and technical specifications, we convert your conceptual designs into market-ready commercial successes.

Global Market Footprint

Our modular surfaces are strategically tailored for modern, residential DIY applications as well as large contract projects. Backed by extensive export compliance, our major target markets include:

North & South America: Capturing the high-growth DIY home improvement and multi-sport court sectors.

The Middle East & Africa: Delivering high-performance, UV-stabilized weather resistance capable of enduring extreme temperature variances.

Southeast Asia: Providing high-efficiency, cost-effective regional deployment and flexible localized supply lines.
