Our Story

A story starting from a warehouse

Five years ago, we were a small team renting half a warehouse in the auto parts city. The daily work is very monotonous: picking up goods from the factory and delivering them to the repair shop. Life goes by, but I always feel like something is missing in my heart.

The turning point occurred in a winter. A customer came to us to buy a batch of wipers and specifically reminded us, 'Last time someone else took them, they rang after two months of use. Can you find some reliable ones for me this time?' We patted our chests and said there was no problem, but this batch of goods still had problems - not all of them, but the repair rate was much higher than our promise.

After that incident, we made a decision: no longer just engage in reverse trading.

02 Satisfy yourself first, then sell it

We started making products ourselves.

Car cables, filter cartridges, tire pressure sensors, wipers, these seemingly ordinary accessories actually have their own requirements. How many tens of thousands of tensile tests are required for the steel wire of the cable to pass? What is the appropriate number of folds and filtration accuracy for filter paper? What is the response threshold of the tire pressure sensor chip? We pick one by one, test one by one.

The process is not easy. Many colleagues around us advise us that it's almost enough, but customers can't tell.

But we know that customers may not be able to see it for a while, but over time, they will be able to see everything. Breaking a component does not mean losing a business deal, but rather the trust of a customer.

From accessories to flooring, the continuation of standards

Later on, we made plastic grid flooring.

The cause is also very simple: our own car washing station's floor grille collapsed after a year of use, cracked in winter, and had poor drainage. I searched around suppliers and found that many products are competing for who is cheaper, with insufficient materials and structural load-bearing capacity.

We have a solid foundation in plastic processing, so we might as well do it ourselves.

From the raw material formula to the grid density, from the inclination angle of the drainage channel to the depth of the anti slip pattern, every detail has been measured and tested again. We will use it ourselves first and confirm that there are no problems before we dare to take it out.

Now, our grid flooring is not only used in car wash stations, but also in outdoor sports fields, around swimming pools, terrace gardens - any place with requirements for drainage and anti slip.

What kind of person do we want to be

We never thought about building a big company.

What we want to do is that one day you go to the repair shop to replace parts, and the boss says, 'You can use this brand with confidence.'; You walked into a car wash and our names were printed on the floor you stepped on.

Since that half warehouse, we have believed in one thing: not to fool people, not to make do with things.
